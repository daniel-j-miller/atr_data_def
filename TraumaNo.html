1.02 Trauma Number


Identifying and definitional attributes

Definition A person identifier unique to the establishment or agency where the person received definitive (final) care.
Justification Collected for administrative purposes, to assist in service provider identification.

Obligation

Desirable


Representational attributes

Guide for use Individual agencies, establishments or collection authorities may use their own alphabetic, numeric or alphanumeric coding systems.
This field may be a hospital medical record (UR) number, or a local trauma registry case number.
